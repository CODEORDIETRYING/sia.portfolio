Implementing Spotify's Wrapped Analytics for a Movie Rental Company using SQL

I implemented the famous Spotify wrapped marketing campaign for a DVD rental company. I leveraged the business' data, making use of complex SQL transformations to prepare personalized marketing copies for each customer. The copies gave customers insight into their watch history (favorite genre, favorite actor), and how they performed compared to other customers in terms of view history.

The copy also provided a simple movie recommendation based on their previously watched genres and movies where their favorite actor stared in.
